FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lumina

How does peer-to-peer energy trading work?▼
Producers list their surplus renewable energy with a price per unit. Consumers browse listings, select a producer, and pay directly — no DISCOM or utility company involved. The energy is measured, verified, and the payment goes straight to the producer's account.
Is Lumina available in my city?▼
Lumina currently supports listings across major Indian cities including Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Chennai, and Hyderabad. We're expanding rapidly — if your city isn't listed yet, sign up and we'll notify you when we arrive.
How is the CO₂ savings calculated?▼
Each energy type has a verified emissions factor — Solar saves 0.82 kg CO₂/kWh, Biogas 0.65 kg/kWh, and Wind 0.92 kg/kWh based on India's average grid emissions intensity. Every transaction automatically calculates your CO₂ offset and updates your dashboard in real time.
What ROI can investors expect?▼
Renewable energy projects on Lumina have historically delivered 10–15% annual returns. Actual ROI depends on the project type, scale, and region. Use the ROI calculator on your Investor Dashboard to model projected returns for any investment amount and duration.
How do I download my Carbon Credit Certificate?▼
After every successful energy purchase, you'll find a gold “Download Carbon Certificate” button on the success page. The certificate is a print-ready document showing your CO₂ offset, transaction ID, energy source, and tree-planting equivalent. It can be saved as a PDF using your browser's print dialog.
Is my payment information secure?▼
Yes. All transactions are processed over encrypted connections. We validate card numbers, expiry dates, and CVV before processing, and we never store raw card data on our servers. Payment details are handled using industry-standard tokenization.
